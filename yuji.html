Abilities

As the vessel of Ryomen Sukuna, Yuji carries immense cursed energy. This power comes at a cost, placing his body and soul in constant danger as he struggles to remain in control.

Abilities

Megumi wields the Ten Shadows Technique, summoning shikigami from shadows to fight on his behalf. Each summon reflects his tactical mindset and adaptability.

Unlike others, Megumi fights with strategy rather than brute force. His potential lies in how far he is willing to push himself when faced with impossible odds.

Abilities

Ryomen Sukuna is an ancient curse feared throughout history. His cursed energy is vast, violent, and destructive, capable of overwhelming even elite sorcerers with ease.

Sukuna’s techniques are brutal and precise, designed purely for domination. His presence alone twists the battlefield, reminding all that he is a king, not a monster.

Abilities

Nobara uses the Straw Doll Technique, attacking enemies through resonance and cursed tools. Her fighting style is aggressive, calculated, and unforgiving.

Nobara draws strength from her confidence and sense of self. She fights without hesitation, proving that resolve can be just as powerful as cursed energy.

Abilities

Nanami wields the Ratio Technique, targeting weak points with mathematical precision. His attacks are efficient, deliberate, and devastating.

Rejecting reckless combat, Nanami values balance and restraint. His strength lies in discipline, making him one of the most reliable sorcerers in battle.
